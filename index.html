ReFoArtist Resources For Artist

ReFoArtist About

Quickposes

is an online tool designed for artists to practice drawing figures and poses. It provides timed drawing exercises with a variety of human reference images to help improve speed, accuracy, and anatomy skills.

RKGK.ORG

is a website designed for artists, particularly those practicing sketching and quick drawing. It offers a variety of pose references, including mannequins, clothed models, and dynamic poses.

SauceNAO

is an image search engine that helps users find the source of images. It is commonly used in online communities, particularly for locating the original source of artwork, anime, manga, and other visual media.

Line of Action

a website designed to help artists improve their drawing skills through practice sessions with timed references. It offers a variety of drawing tools and resources, including figure drawing, animal drawing, and facial expression references, enabling artists to hone their technique and build a solid foundation in anatomy and gesture drawing.

AdorkaStock Sketch

versatile online resource designed for artists looking to improve their drawing skills, particularly in dynamic poses and figure drawing. The website offers an extensive library of high-quality reference images featuring a variety of poses, angles, and lighting conditions.

Posemaniacs

website that provides a collection of 3D models in various poses, designed to assist artists in studying anatomy and practicing figure drawing.
